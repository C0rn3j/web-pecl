Zend Engine 2 and Errors

Two things came up about the move towards PHP5 and Zend Engine 2, the new catch throw error reporting and the new reserved keyword 'delete', Stig has been testing ZE2 and the new raiseError method look like it will be simplified considerably....., On the delete front, It was noticed that DB_DBA and DB_DataObject both used delete methods, this would break in PHP5. Brent changed the delete method to remove in DB_DBA, Alan meanwhile started a brief discussion with Andi to see if delete could be allowed as a class method..
